  • Pats Peak Mountain

PATS PEAK MOUNTAIN, a charming and family-friendly skiing destination, is located in the beautiful town of Henniker, New Hampshire. This beloved local gem offers a delightful skiing experience for all skill levels with its 28 well-groomed trails, spanning 115 acres of skiable terrain. The mountain's 1,460-foot summit elevation and a 770-foot vertical drop provide a diverse mix of slopes, from gentle beginner-friendly runs to adrenaline-pumping expert trails. Pats Peak is known for its dependable snowmaking capabilities, ensuring excellent snow conditions throughout the winter season. Additionally, its efficient lift system, including a high-speed quad chairlift, makes accessing the slopes a breeze. Visitors can take advantage of the ski and snowboard school, equipment rentals, and a welcoming base lodge featuring a range of dining and entertainment options. With its warm and inviting atmosphere, Pats Peak Ski Mountain is the ideal destination for families and winter sports enthusiasts looking to create lasting memories on the slopes.
