  • Wachusett Mountain

Wachusett Mountain, located in Princeton, Massachusetts, is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. Boasting an elevation of 2,006 feet, the mountain provides breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape, including the Boston skyline on clear days. As a part of the Wachusett Mountain State Reservation, the area offers a wide variety of recreational activities such as hiking, skiing, and wildlife observation. The mountain is home to Wachusett Mountain Ski Area, a well-known winter sports destination in Central Massachusetts, featuring 27 trails catering to skiers and snowboarders of all skill levels. Throughout the year, visitors can explore over 17 miles of picturesque hiking trails, traversing diverse terrains, and meandering through lush forests, rocky outcrops, and serene meadows. Rich in history and natural beauty, Wachusett Mountain serves as a scenic getaway for those seeking adventure, relaxation, and an escape from the bustling urban life.
