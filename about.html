SKIFIND is a ski mountain comparison site for the New England area mountains. The purpose for this website is to make it easier for you to compare different ski mountains with different criteria. There are 76 mountains in New England and sometimes it is difficult to figure out what mountain is the best. We will help you decide your next adventure spot depending on your needs.
