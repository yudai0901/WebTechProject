Project Proposal: Yudai Yamakawa

New England Ski Mountain Comparison Site

What is your website about?

The website is a ski mountain review site, where users can compare mountains in the New England area side by side to decide which mountain to go up to for the weekend.

What is the objective of your website?

The objective for this website is to make it easier for skiers to compare different ski mountains with different criteria. There are 76 mountains in New England and sometimes it is difficult to figure out what mountain is the best. This website will help users decide your mountain for the weekend depending on your needs such as bars, EV changing, and location.
