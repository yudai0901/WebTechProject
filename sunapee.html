  • Sunapee Mountain

SUNAPEE MOUNTAIN, nestled in the heart of New Hampshire's picturesque Dartmouth-Lake Sunapee Region, is a premier ski destination that attracts winter sports enthusiasts from around the world. Boasting over 230 acres of skiable terrain, it features 66 diverse trails and glades to accommodate skiers and snowboarders of all skill levels. With a summit elevation of 2,743 feet and a vertical drop of 1,510 feet, Sunapee Mountain offers a blend of gentle slopes and challenging descents, all serviced by 10 state-of-the-art lifts, including high-speed quads and a triple chair. The mountain's snowmaking capabilities, coupled with an average annual snowfall of 100 inches, ensure optimal conditions throughout the season. At the base of the mountain, visitors can find a welcoming lodge offering amenities such as equipment rentals, dining options, and a ski school, making Sunapee Mountain the ultimate destination for an unforgettable winter adventure.
