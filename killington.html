  • Killington Mountain

KILINGTON MOUNTAIN, nestled in the heart of the Green Mountains in central Vermont, stands as a breathtaking destination for outdoor enthusiasts and adventure seekers. Renowned as the "Beast of the East," this impressive peak boasts over 1,500 acres of skiable terrain, including six interconnected mountain areas, making it the largest ski resort in Eastern North America. With a summit elevation of 4,241 feet and featuring more than 150 trails, Killington Mountain caters to all skill levels, from novice to expert. During the warmer months, the mountain transforms into a paradise for hikers, mountain bikers, and golfers, with scenic trails and stunning vistas at every turn. The natural beauty, diverse range of outdoor activities, and the lively base village create an unforgettable experience for visitors year-round.
